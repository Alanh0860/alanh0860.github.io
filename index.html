Jeweler Studio

Jeweler Studio is the company I’m building: an AI design studio for custom jewelers. A customer describes a piece, and a photo-real render appears in seconds, priced and ready to order, right on the jeweler’s website.

What used to take weeks of sketches and emails now takes seconds. A customer finally sees their idea before they buy it, and the jeweler closes the sale while the excitement is still there. Watching a sentence turn into a photo-real ring has not gotten old.

I build all of it: the product, the renders, and the business. It’s the hardest and most absorbing thing I’ve made, and I think about it constantly.
