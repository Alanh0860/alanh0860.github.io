# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Anyone Alan hands his name to: a prospective collaborator, a new friend, a curious
stranger who got the link. They arrive knowing little, usually on a phone in the evening
or a laptop between things, and leave with a vivid sense of who Alan is. The job is
introduction, not conversion. There is nothing to buy and nothing to sign up for.

## Product Purpose

A personal landing page that introduces Alan through the two halves of his life: going
*out* into the world (skydiving, rock climbing, marathons, triathlons, cliff jumping,
travel) and coming *home* to make things and gather people (baking, cooking, hosting
dinner parties, photography). Success is a visitor who finishes the scroll feeling like
they have met someone specific, and who remembers the page a day later.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, warm, and quietly daring. The voice is first-person, plainspoken, and
specific. It states what Alan actually did and where, never reaching for adjectives the
photo already earns. Three words: **adventurous, hospitable, composed.**

## Anti-references

- The cream / sand / parchment editorial-magazine landing page. The single most common
  AI-personal-site look of 2026. Warm near-white background, italic display serif,
  tiny tracked uppercase eyebrows over every section, ruled three-column metadata.
- The SaaS "hero metric" stat bar: big gradient number, small label, three supporting
  figures in a row. Alan's numbers are a logbook, not a dashboard.
- The centered-name-over-a-photo-grid template with a row of social icons.
- Generic stock photography. Every image here is Alan's own.

## Design Principles

1. **The photograph is the design.** Imagery leads; type and layout frame it. When a
   photo can carry the moment, get out of its way.
2. **Two worlds, one voice.** Horizons (out) and Hands (home) get different light and
   temperature, but the same restraint. Consistency of voice over consistency of treatment.
3. **Earn every motion.** One orchestrated hero reveal, plus motion that fits exactly
   what it reveals. No uniform fade-on-scroll reflex. Everything readable without JS and
   with reduced motion.
4. **Specific beats impressive.** Real place names, real distances, real handles. The
   detail is the luxury.
5. **Fast and hand-built.** No framework, no tracker, no library bloat. Speed and
   accessibility are part of the craft an experienced eye will notice.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Body text ≥ 4.5:1, large text ≥ 3:1 on every surface (verified on the
dark base and the warm Table section). Full keyboard path with visible focus, a skip link,
semantic landmarks, and descriptive alt text written in Alan's voice. Every animation has a
`prefers-reduced-motion` alternative, and all content is visible without JavaScript.
